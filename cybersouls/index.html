All are in the shape of flames but the real flame is in fire’s colour is the Faithfriend to whom the Cybersouls come to depend.

The overall appearance shows a hand holding a precious pearl in fingers. The pearl is nothing but the faith Cybersouls hold to their souls always. The letter ‘Y’ is in another font which gives a look of the Holy One of God.

  • Where it is

    This project evolved as part of a PhD research project in Mater Dei Institute of Education, Dublin, Ireland.

    This project is supported by the Office of Evangelisation of the Archdiocese of Dublin.

    As evangelisation of young adults on the Internet is the topic of this innovative research, hence this is an online project.

    Faith Friends of this project are authorised by a Catholic youth movement.

  • How it works

    A safe environment is provided where young adults are invited to register and logon to cybersouls.ie.

    After you logon you will be introduced to a Faith Friend. Faithfriends belong to a Catholic movement and have received training to accompany you spiritually online.

    While they accompany you online, they will share various creative and interesting spiritual themes with you. These themes are called scrolls, originally from the Dead Sea Scrolls.

    Based on these themes, discussions will be initiated between you and your Faith Friend. This will help deepen your faith.

  • Glossary

    Cybersoul – Anybody (18 and over) who wants to deepen faith online in this project will be called a cybersoul. They have to register and logon to begin this journey.

    Faithfriend – A Faithfriend is authorised by a Catholic youth movement and they will be accompanying a cybersoul in this journey of faith online.

    Scrolls – A term used for referring to short Catholic themes shared by a Faithfriend with a Cybersoul in this project. The term ‘scrolls’ comes from Dead Sea Scrolls which are ancient manuscripts of the Bible.

The Project

The Project: Cybersouls is a project for young people. Its aim is to develop faith through online networking. It is designed to develop the faith of young people through their online interaction with Faithfriends . ‘Cyebrsouls’ is a term given to those who want to initiate their faith journey online while Faithfriends, primarily authorised by the Jesus Youth movement, would accompany Cybersouls on their journey. Here, either you are a Cybersoul or a Faithfriend.

The Research

The Research: The project started as part of a PhD research in Mater Institute of Education, Dublin, Ireland. Fr Sony Sebastian, CMI, a research student of the Institute, is the principal investigator. The data obtained during the first six months of the project is expected to be used for the research.
