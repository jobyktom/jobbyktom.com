Welcome to The Boulevard

The Boulevard is a delightful family restaurant situated in the pleasant seaside resort of East Wittering in West Sussex. The business, originally set up in 1979 is now run by the second generation of the same family. Serving the local village as well as visitors and holiday makers, The Boulevard offers a wide range of delicious home-cooked meals from traditional Fish and Chips and Sunday Roasts to daily specials which include locally caught fresh fish such including crab and lobster when in season.

Fully licensed restaurant with an extensive wine list
Open for breakfast, lunch and dinner 7 days a week
Plated meals as well as takeaway service available (no deliveries)
Daily changing blackboard of specials
High chairs available
Baby changing facilities
Wi-Fi service
Major credit cards accepted

We are sorry but we cannot take bookings over the phone or by email. Tables are available on a first come first served basis and we will always do our very best to seat you as soon as possible. If you have a large party of over 10 people, it might be an idea to let us know you are coming so we can make some prior arrangements in order to seat you together. However, during the extremely busy summer months, we are unable to guarantee this service.

Did you know we also have another restaurant in Selsey, The Riveira offering the same friendly service, good food and excellent value?
